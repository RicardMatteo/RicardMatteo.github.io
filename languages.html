English meeting :

During the course of this semester, we are responsible for holding meetings on a subject of our choice. This requires that we use our organizational skills and to assert ourselves as leaders to ensure that the necessary tasks are accomplished.

Additionally, in a meeting hosted by another student, our active listening skills are tested. Indeed, it is necessary to write and filter each person's ideas to present a minute of this meeting.

Even if the topics of the meetings are not necessarily serious, these meetings help us a lot to understand the functioning of a real meeting within a company. And I already saw the importance of making an agenda and taking a minute since I am the treasurer of a club at ENSEEIHT and we meet regularly to move forward with our projects.

I will be chairing my meeting the 25th of November and the topics will be the seven deadly sins.
